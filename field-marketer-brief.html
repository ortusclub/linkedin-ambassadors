First, what's LinkedVelocity?

LinkedVelocity is a marketplace where professionals earn passive income by sharing a LinkedIn account they're not actively using — vetted businesses rent it for outreach, and the owner gets paid monthly. We handle all the security, access and support. The people who share their accounts are our “ambassadors.”

Your job: get more ambassadors signed up.

Why LinkedVelocity exists

Think of LinkedIn as Facebook, but for professionals — it's where businesses, buyers and decision-makers all are. That's why, in sales and marketing, it's the #1 place to find clients: a message on LinkedIn gets seen and trusted far more than a cold email or phone call.

Here's how businesses use it: they reach out to potential clients — connection requests and messages — and a share of those turn into deals. The rule is simple: the more people you can reach, the more clients you win.

But there's a hard ceiling. LinkedIn limits how much one account can reach out each week (it used to allow far more). Push past it and the account gets restricted. So a single account can only bring in so many clients — no matter how good the business is. And the obvious fix, spinning up more accounts, doesn't work: new or fake profiles get flagged instantly and nobody trusts them. What businesses need is more real, established accounts.

💡 Quick example: if a business can reach ~100 people a week from one account, it needs 5 accounts to reach 500. It can't just create 5 — they'd get banned — so it rents 5 real ones. Each of those is someone a field marketer like you signed up.

That's LinkedVelocity: we connect businesses that want to grow with everyday professionals who have a solid LinkedIn they're not really using. The business scales safely across real accounts; the owner earns passive income for it. Your job is to bring in those account owners.

What counts as a quality sign-up

Not everyone's a fit — the best sign-ups are real, established professionals, not brand-new or empty accounts. Those are the ones businesses actually want to rent, so they're the ones that get onboarded (and earn you the ₱500). Aim for people whose LinkedIn has:

  • An established account — ideally around a year old or more, not freshly created.
  • 100+ connections — 300+ is even better.
  • A real, complete profile — photo, job title, some activity.
  • Verified — a nice bonus, but not required.
✅ Rule of thumb: if they look like an active professional who's been on LinkedIn a while, they're a great fit.

“Wait — is this legit?” (you'll get asked this)

It's the main thing people want reassurance on. Here's why they're comfortable:

  • They're always in control. They can pause or take their account back anytime — nothing's permanent.
  • Their password is never shared. Access is secure and fully managed by LinkedVelocity — the renter never sees their login.
  • It's completely passive. They earn every month from a network they already built and aren't actively using — we handle everything.
  • It's a real, established business with paying customers and a proper support team behind it.
🗣️ Your simple answer: “You stay in full control and your login is never shared — you just earn each month from a LinkedIn you're barely using, and we handle the rest.”
